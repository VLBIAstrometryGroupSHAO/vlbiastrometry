Opportunities

Graduated Student Positions

If you are interested in pursuing either Master or Ph.D. degree in our group, please feel free to contact any of us or directly to Prof. Zhang for detials. Financial support in various forms is available. Shanghai Astronomical Observatory usually provides strongly subsidized dormitory housing for graduate students.

无论你是大一大二的新生，还是大三大四有意愿进入研究生深造阶段的同学，VLBI天体测量课题组非常欢迎感兴趣的同学申请加入、保研或报考我们课题组。
